About Me

Hello, I'm Cheng Zheng! I received the Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from University of California, Los Angeles. I was advised by Professor Wei Wang. My research interests include graph mining, social network analysis and deep learning.

What's New

  • Sep. 2020: I defended my Ph.D. dissertation and graduated from UCLA.!
  • July 2020: Multiple papers get accepted to CIKM, SIGIR, ECML-PKDD.
  • May 2020: Our paper Robust Graph Representation Learning via Neural Sparsification gets accepted to ICML 2020!
  • May 2018: Our paper Learning Deep Network Representations with Adversarially Regularized Autoencoders gets accepted to Research Track in KDD 2018!
